Research : The Maison de la Simulation is a research laboratory about numerical simulation. Those activites concern transverse fields like IT and visualization, as well as HPC.

Project : The Maison de la Simulation is involved in projects for research and supporting scientific community purpose : tools development, ANR, European projects ...

Training course : Information on all HPC training proposed by Maison de la Simulation. These trainings are organized in the context of the French PATC (PRACE Advanced Training Center), with collaborators like IDRIS, Equip@meso or Groupe Calcul.
